How to Play:

  • Click 'New Game' to start a new game or reset to a new game.
  • Click 'Roll Dice' to roll the dice for each round of play.

Game Rules:

  • There are 3 rounds of play for each game.
  • The score for each round is the sum of the two dice values.
  • However, if a player rolls 1 for any of their two dice, that player scores 0 for that round.
  • Also, if a player rolls a pair of the same number (other than 1), the player's score doubles for that round.
  • The highest cumulative total score after 3 rounds wins the game!
